Coursework

Hey whats up, fyi this webpage is under construction. I'm undergrad at UC Berkeley, where I study math and biology. I made this web page in order to highlight my research, coursework and cool stuff I've done.

Currently, I work in Adam Arkin's lab on the NASA CUBES project, and I focus on the design, modeling and control of microbial communities for enhanced agricultural activity. Before I worked in the Arkin lab, I worked in Jay Keasling's lab at the Joint Bio Energy Institute to improve Pseudomonas putida as a microbial chassis for the production of biofuels and commodity chemicals from biomass waste.

Outside of these research groups I have interned at Senti biosciences, where I tested miRNA and CART circuits in various human cell lines. Additionally, I have also done some independent research with a team that won third place in the UC Big Ideas project by proposing directed evolution of a pseudomonas strain which degrades polyethylene.
